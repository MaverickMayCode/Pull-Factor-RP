WHAT WE DON'T ENFORCE

Freedom means fewer rules. We focus on fun, not bureaucracy.

  • No job restrictions - be creative, community demotes bad actors
  • No strict RDM rules - minor conflicts happen, major abuse gets vote banned
  • No NLR - come back and fight, just don't mass RDM

CRIME TRACKER SYSTEM

Our advanced police system prevents arrest abuse and rewards active law enforcement!

  • Earn CP Points for arrests and successful scans
  • Spend points at the Police Equipment Vendor for weapons, armor, and cash
  • Scanner required for arrests - no more random arrests!
